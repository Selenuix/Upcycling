Description of the project:

So, we are working on Recycling and Upcycling at school. Teachers told us to take an object and upcycle it into a new and cool object. I looked at the stuff I had at home and I decided to choose cassets. I think that everyone had cassets at home, if it's not the case or your grandparents will probably give you somes your grandparents will probably give you somes. But now, the problem is to find a way to upcycle these cassets. The only solution that I found, was to create a pencil holder.

Estimated time:

This project is really simple to do. You can create your own pencil holder in about 20 minutes (including time needed to heat the glue gun).

Materials you will need:

To make your own pencil holder, you will need :

  • a sharp knife,
  • glue gun
  • salvaged cardboard
  • scissors

Step One:

Take and cut cardboard, to the dimensions you need using the sharp knife.

Step 2:

Take a cassette and glue it to the cardboard.

Step 3:

Take another cassette and glue it to the cardboard and the other one.

Step 4:

Repeat the operation with the two others.

Result:

Your pencil holder is now ready !
